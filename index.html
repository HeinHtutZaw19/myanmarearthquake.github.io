Displaced by Earthquakes, Pressured by Crisis

After losing their homes to devastating earthquakes, civilians are now forced to survive in makeshift tents with no safety net—no insurance, no protection. As if that weren’t enough, heavy rainfall and winds have made the tents infeasible. Meanwhile, prices for basic necessities keep rising, making even food and medicine unaffordable. Families are caught between natural disaster and daily crisis, struggling every day just to survive.

Crisis After Crisis

  • Mar 2021: Economic Instability – Food and fuel prices surged while salary stays the same. Source
  • Sep 2024: Historic Flooding – The worst floods in 50 years displaced thousands. Source
  • Mar 2025: Major Earthquake – A 7.7 magnitude quake leaving 4,500 dead, and many displaced. Source
